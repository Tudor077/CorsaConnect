Your phone is the wheel.

Tilt your Android phone to steer, tap the on-screen pedals and shifter, and watch a live speed / RPM / gear dashboard streamed straight from the game. Your phone shows up as an ordinary Xbox controller, so it works in any game.

Needs the free ViGEmBus driver and a phone on the same Wi-Fi as your PC.

Tilt to steer

The gyroscope turns your phone into a wheel. Calibrate the center once and steer with no drift.

Live dashboard

Speed, RPM and gear stream from the game in real time, with analog or digital gauges and a redline that auto-fits each car.

Build your own HUD

Drag, resize and rebind every pedal, button and gauge. Lay out the cockpit exactly how you like it.

Just a controller

A virtual Xbox 360 pad means no special game support. If the game can read a gamepad, it works.

🪟 On the PC

Install ViGEmBus, run CorsaConnect.exe, and press Launch. SmartScreen may warn for new apps, choose More info → Run anyway. If Windows asks about the firewall, choose Allow so the phone can connect.

🎮 In your game

Bind the Xbox pad in the game's controls. For the live dashboard in BeamNG: Options → Other → Protocols, enable OutGauge (and OutSim for feedback) on IP 127.0.0.1.

🤖 On Android

Open the .apk and allow installs when prompted. Type the IP the launcher shows, tap Connect, hold the phone like a wheel and Center.
